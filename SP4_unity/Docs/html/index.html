CS: Fog of War

A third-person multiplayer survival game where infinite waves of enemies will come after you. You have to protect yourself while defending and doing the objectives.

Players control vehicles with different weapons for each vehicle.

Inspired by: Minigore, Helldivers, Grim Dawn (Crucible DLC)

Team members

Github

https://github.com/eenvictus/SP4

master: non-networking branch

multiplayer-master-2: networking branch

How to play

W - drive forward
A - steer left
S - reverse
D - steer right
Space - break
P - Re-orientate
Left click - shoot in cursor direction
